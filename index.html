Process

Our Asset Management process ensures efficient allocation, movement, auditing, and retirement of assets through a structured workflow:

  • Asset Allocation: Goods are received, verified for compliance, tagged, stored, and allocated to users, cost centers, or locations after installation and quality checks.
  • Intra Unit Transfer: Assets can be transferred within the same site, managed by Operations or IT Managers, and coordinated with logistics for shifting.
  • Inter Unit Transfer: Assets are moved across sites with proper approvals and compliance, ensuring tracking for permanent or temporary transfers.
  • Asset Audits: Periodic audits are performed to scan and reconcile assets, resolve discrepancies, and ensure compliance and regularization.
  • Retirement: Assets are disposed through a structured approval process including selling, scrapping, or writing off, with proper documentation and updates to registers.

Features & Implementation Steps

Marking office topography

Mapping and documenting the complete layout of office premises to identify asset placement and operational zones.

Labelling and extracting asset details

Assigning unique identification tags to each asset and recording key information for management.

Categorizing all assets to standard 5 levels

Classifying assets into five structured levels for easier tracking, auditing, and reporting across the organization.

Collating all assets with purchase details

Linking each asset to purchase invoices and financial records for accurate verification and audit compliance.

Listing all vendors and their profiles Linking them to the relevant assets

Maintaining a database of vendors and mapping them to supplied assets for seamless service and procurement management.

Listing all service providers and their profiles Linking them to the relevant assets

Recording service providers and associating them with respective assets for maintenance and lifecycle management.

Organization structure/employee profile

Defining a clear organizational hierarchy to establish accountability for asset usage. Employee profiles include asset ownership details compliance with internal policies.

Finalizing

Conducting a final review of all asset data, cross-verifying with invoices, vendor records, and audit findings. Ensuring compliance with regulatory standards, updating the central asset register, and locking data for operational use.
